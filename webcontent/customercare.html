Shipping & Returns

COMPLIMENTARY SHIPPING

Maison Vyra offers complimentary standard shipping on all orders globally. Each fragrance is meticulously hand-packaged in our signature black and gold gifting presentation to ensure safe transit.

DELIVERY TIMEFRAMES

Standard Delivery: 3–5 Business Days
Express Delivery: 1–2 Business Days (Additional charges apply at checkout)

RETURN POLICY

We accept returns of unopened, unused items in their original cellophane wrapping within 30 days of delivery. Due to the personal nature of our products and hygiene standards, we cannot accept returns on bottles that have been unsealed or sprayed. To initiate a return, please utilize the Contact form to request a prepaid return label.

Privacy Policy

At Maison Vyra, protecting the privacy and security of our esteemed clients is of paramount importance. This brief outlines how we handle your data.

INFORMATION COLLECTION

We collect necessary information such as your name, billing address, shipping address, payment information, email address, and phone number solely to fulfill your order and provide an exceptional bespoke experience.

DATA SECURITY

Your payment data is encrypted using high-level SSL technology. We do not store your credit card details on our servers after a transaction is complete.

COOKIE POLICY

We utilize cookies to tailor your browsing experience, remember your cart items, and understand client preferences. You may adjust your browser settings to decline cookies, though this may impact site functionality.
